# Sales Agencies Niche -- Copy Blocklist Additions

Niche-specific banned terms and phrases that extend the universal blocklist at
`website-factory/references/copy/ai-vocab-blocklist.md`.

These terms appear either in low-quality competitor agency copy or in language patterns
that signal low confidence to the buyer in this niche.

---

## Niche-specific banned terms

**Agency self-promotion language:**
- "full-service sales solutions"
- "comprehensive sales ecosystem"
- "end-to-end sales journey"
- "360-degree sales approach"
- "bespoke sales methodology"
- "data-driven insights" (use specific data instead: "22 qualified meetings in 30 days")
- "proven track record" (prove it with numbers instead)
- "industry-leading" (show the Clutch rank instead)
- "best-in-breed" (meaningless without comparison)
- "go-to-market strategy" (overused; say what you actually build)

**Weak guarantees / weasel language:**
- "we aim to"
- "we strive to"
- "we try to"
- "we look to deliver"
- "results may vary"
- "subject to client engagement"
- "depending on your market"
- "no guarantees in sales"

**Generic CTA language:**
- "contact us today"
- "reach out"
- "don't hesitate to contact us"
- "we'd love to hear from you"
- "get in touch"
- "drop us a line"

**B2B jargon that sounds like every other agency:**
- "pipeline velocity" (use "meetings booked per week" instead)
- "demand generation" (say "outbound leads")
- "revenue acceleration" (say "more closed deals faster")
- "go-to-market motion" (say "how we sell")
- "top of funnel activities" (say "outreach")
- "solutions" as a noun standing alone

---

## Niche-specific preferred terms

Use these instead:

| Avoid | Use instead |
|---|---|
| "business development" | "sales" or "outbound" |
| "pipeline velocity" | "meetings booked per week" |
| "demand generation" | "qualified outbound leads" |
| "revenue acceleration" | "more closed deals" |
| "solutions" | name the actual service |
| "proven track record" | specific client outcome with a number |
| "data-driven" | specific metric |
| "get in touch" | "Book a Strategy Call" |

---

## Confirmation message anti-patterns

Never use in post-form confirmation:
- "Thank you for reaching out to us."
- "A member of our team will be in touch shortly."
- "We appreciate your interest."
- "Your message has been received."
- "We will get back to you as soon as possible."

Use instead: "You're booked. Check your email for a calendar confirmation." (Per copy-locks.json thankYouMessage.)
