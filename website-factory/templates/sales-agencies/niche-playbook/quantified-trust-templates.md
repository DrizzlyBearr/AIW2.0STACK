# Sales Agencies Niche -- Quantified Trust Statement Templates

Used by Stage 6 copy-deck agent to write trust claims with real client numbers.
Replace [bracketed] placeholders with per-client values from brand-dna.json.

---

## Stats-bar templates

Use these for the 4-cell stats bar immediately below the hero.

1. "[N]+ qualified meetings booked for clients"
2. "[N]+ B2B companies served"
3. "[N] industries where we have built pipeline"
4. "[X] stars on Clutch ([N] verified reviews)"
5. "[N]+ SDR campaigns launched"
6. "[X]% average reply rate across client campaigns"

Pick 4. Prioritize the most impressive number the client can genuinely claim. Do not fabricate numbers.

## Risk-reversal statement templates

Choose the version that matches the actual guarantee the client is prepared to honor.

**Full guarantee:**
"Most clients see their first qualified meeting within 30 days. If we reach the end of month two without a single meeting booked, we extend at no cost until we hit the target. You pay for outcomes, not time."

**Partial guarantee (minimum commitment):**
"Engagements run on a 60-day minimum. If you reach the end of month two without a single qualified meeting, we will extend the program at no additional cost for a further 30 days."

**Performance-based framing (no formal guarantee):**
"Our commercial model is built on repeatable results. If the pipeline is not moving, we diagnose and fix the playbook. We have never let a client reach month three without a qualified conversation."
