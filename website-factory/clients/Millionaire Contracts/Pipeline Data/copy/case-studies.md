## Case Study 1: Skayle 360

**Industry:** B2B SaaS
**Stat:** 120 qualified appointments booked

**The situation:**
Skayle 360 had a product with clear product-market fit and no outbound system. The founder was doing every prospecting call personally. Revenue was growing, but the ceiling was approaching fast. They could not scale beyond what the founder could personally handle.

**The problem:**
No ICP document. No outreach sequences. No rep placement process. Every deal started with a founder introduction and moved at founder speed. The business needed to sell without the founder in every conversation.

**What we built:**
We started with a week of ICP definition and playbook build. We documented the buyer profile, the discovery framework, and the objection set. Then we built the outbound infrastructure: CRM configuration, cold email sequences, LinkedIn outreach logic, and lead list build against the defined ICP.

Once the system was live, we placed a briefed SDR and launched outreach.

**The result:**
120 qualified appointments booked. The founder stepped out of prospecting and into closing. Outreach continued without founder involvement.

## Case Study 2: McLifestyle Fitness / Medellin Health

**Industry:** Health and Wellness
**Stat:** 2.4x conversion improvement

**The situation:**
McLifestyle Fitness and its associated entity Medellin Health were running a DM-based outreach pipeline with no documented system. Leads were coming in through social channels but going cold before any follow-up reached them. The conversion rate was low and inconsistent.

**The problem:**
No follow-up sequence. No reply handling logic. No pipeline stage structure. Prospects expressed interest and then heard nothing for days. By the time the team followed up, the lead had moved on.

**What we built:**
We built a DM outreach system with structured follow-up sequences, reply handling logic, and pipeline stage criteria. Every inbound lead hit a defined path: first response within a set window, follow-up sequence if no reply, qualification criteria before the call was booked.

**The result:**
Conversion rate improved 2.4x. More leads moved from initial contact to booked call. The system ran without daily founder intervention.
