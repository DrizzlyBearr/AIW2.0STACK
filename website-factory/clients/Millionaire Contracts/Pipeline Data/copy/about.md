## Founder Section

**Label:** The founder

**Heading:** Katleho Mohlomi

**Para 1:**
Before founding Millionaire Contracts, Katleho built outbound sales infrastructure across more than 15 B2B clients. The engagements covered SaaS, health and wellness, legal, fintech, private equity, web agencies, and recruitment. Clients included Skayle 360, Eads Bridge Holdings, and iLawyer Marketing. The work was the same in every engagement: define the ICP, build the playbook, configure the outbound system, and manage the pipeline until the founder stopped being the only salesperson.

**Para 2:**
At some point, the pattern became obvious. The founders who struggled were not struggling because they lacked a sales rep. They were struggling because they had not built the system the rep needed to run on. The rep was arriving before the infrastructure. Millionaire Contracts is the answer to that problem: a managed service that builds the system first and places the rep second.

**Vision label:** Why we exist

**Vision:** Most founder-led businesses have a ceiling imposed by the founder's personal selling capacity. That ceiling is not a product problem or a market problem. It is a system problem. We remove it.

**Mission label:** How we work

**Mission:** Every engagement starts with the playbook. No rep touches a prospect until the ICP is defined, the discovery framework is documented, and the objection set is mapped. Then the system goes live, the rep is placed, and the pipeline runs.
