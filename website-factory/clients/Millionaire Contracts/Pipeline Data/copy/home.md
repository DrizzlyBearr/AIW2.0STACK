## FAQ Section

**Heading:** Frequently asked questions.

Q: How quickly can we get started?
A: Most clients are live within 14 days. Week one is playbook build and rep briefing. Week two is outreach launch. First qualified conversations typically appear in week two or three.

Q: Do I need to have a sales process in place before we begin?
A: No. Building the process is the first thing we do. Week one is ICP definition, discovery framework, objection scripting, and sequence build. The rep does not touch prospects until the playbook is done.

Q: What industries do you work with?
A: We have built sales systems for clients in SaaS, health and wellness, legal, fintech, private equity, web agencies, and recruitment. Every engagement starts with a custom ICP build, so the industry is a starting point, not a constraint.

Q: What is your guarantee?
A: If month two ends without a single meeting booked, we extend the engagement at no cost until we hit the target. The guarantee is in writing before we start.

Q: How much time will this take from me?
A: Week one requires your input: ICP definition, discovery call review, objection mapping. After that, you receive a weekly Monday report and have live CRM access. We handle reply management, meeting scheduling, and sequence adjustments. Your job is to show up to the qualified meetings.

Q: What happens if a rep isn't performing?
A: Rep performance is our responsibility, not yours. If a placed rep is not hitting sequence targets, we replace them. You are not managing the rep. We are.

Q: Do you publish pricing?
A: We do not publish pricing because every engagement is scoped to your business: the ICP, the outreach channels, the rep profile, and the pipeline complexity. Book a strategy call and we will scope the engagement before we talk numbers.
