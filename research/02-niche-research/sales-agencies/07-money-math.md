### What the Typical SMB Deal Looks Like

A founder-led business with $500K-$5M ARR, buying managed outbound for the first time, typically starts at:
- Setup fee: $2,000-$5,000 (one-time)
- Monthly retainer: $4,000-$8,000/month
- Contract length: 3-6 months minimum (most agencies), some offer month-to-month with higher retainer
- Total first-year spend: $50,000-$100,000

For Millionaire Contracts (MC) as a client example: MC's positioning as a "sales infrastructure" company (not just SDR outsourcing) means clients are likely paying for a more comprehensive engagement. Estimated retainer range: $5,000-$15,000/month depending on scope (rep placement + playbook + systems setup).

## Estimated Close Rate per Qualified Lead

Based on Kondo B2B Sales Benchmarks 2025 and industry data:

- Average B2B win rate (across all company sizes): approximately 20-21% (HubSpot 2024 Sales Trends Report)
- Average B2B win rate for SMB-focused offers: approximately 25-30% (SMB deals close faster and have fewer stakeholders)
- Qualified discovery call to proposal: approximately 50-70% conversion
- Proposal to close: approximately 30-40% for high-trust service businesses with strong social proof

For a sales agency with strong case studies and Clutch reviews, a reasonable working assumption:
- 10 discovery calls per month
- 6 advance to proposal (60%)
- 2-3 close (30-35% close rate on proposals)
- 2-3 new clients per month from 10 qualified discovery calls

## Leads Needed per Month for Meaningful Revenue

Defining "meaningful" as 2 new clients per month at an average monthly retainer of $6,000:

| Target | Math |
|---|---|
| Revenue target: $12,000 MRR from new clients | 2 clients x $6,000 |
| Proposals needed to close 2 (35% close): | 6 proposals |
| Discovery calls needed to produce 6 proposals (60% advance): | 10 qualified calls |
| Leads (website inquiries) needed to produce 10 qualified calls (40% qualify): | 25 leads |

This means a sales agency website needs to generate approximately 25 qualified form submissions or inbound inquiries per month to close 2 new clients at a $6,000 retainer. For a $10,000 retainer, the same 10-call, 6-proposal math produces $20,000/month MRR from new clients.

### Cost of acquisition comparison

Outsourced SDR approach to generate those 25 leads:
- At $300/qualified appointment: 25 qualified calls x $300 = $7,500/month in acquisition cost
- CAC per client: $7,500 / 2 new clients = $3,750
- LTV at $6,000/month with 12-month average retention: $72,000
- LTV:CAC ratio: 19:1 (healthy)

Website as organic lead gen:
- One-time website investment: $5,000-$15,000
- Monthly SEO/content investment: $1,000-$3,000
- If website generates 25 qualified leads/month after 3-6 months of SEO: near-zero per-lead cost
- CAC from organic: effectively $0 after investment payback
