# 07 - Money Math: ROI of a Law Firm Website

Research date: 2026-05-21
Niche: Small to mid-size law firms (1-20 attorneys)
End customer perspective: The law firm (Katleho's client) is the buyer of the website. The ROI pitch is made to the law firm partner or managing attorney.

---

## Average Case Value by Practice Area

### Personal Injury
- Average settlement per case: ~$55,000 (based on 5,861+ cases, 2021-2024)
- Median settlement: ~$48,855
- Attorney contingency fee: 33-40% of settlement
- Attorney revenue per average PI case: $18,000 to $22,000
- High-value PI cases (TBI, spinal cord, wrongful death): $100,000 to $1,000,000+ in attorney fees
- Car accident average settlement: $15,000 to $75,000 (attorney earns $5,000 to $30,000)
- Medical malpractice average settlement: $100,000 to $1,000,000+ (attorney earns $33,000 to $400,000+)

Source: Tribeca Lawsuit Loans, CasePeer, Mattiacci Law, Siegfried & Jensen, Roden Law analysis

**Practical per-case revenue for a small PI firm:** $5,000 to $25,000 per case depending on case type and severity. A reasonable planning assumption for a small firm's average closed case is $12,000 to $15,000 in attorney revenue.

---

### Family Law / Divorce
- Average total attorney fees per divorce case: $7,000 to $20,000+
- Uncontested divorce with a lawyer: ~$4,100
- Contested divorce (at least one trial issue): ~$20,400
- Multi-issue contested divorce: ~$23,300
- Average hourly rate: $150 to $500 (Clio average: $344/hour)
- Average retainer: $2,000 to $10,000+ upfront

Source: Nolo, DivorceNet, aiLawyer, Custody Xchange, Leaders in Law

**Practical per-case revenue for a small family law firm:** $4,000 to $20,000 depending on case complexity. Uncontested cases are lower margin but higher volume. Contested custody or asset division cases are higher value.

---

### Criminal Defense
- Misdemeanor flat fee: $1,500 to $7,500
- Felony retainer: $5,000 to $50,000+
- Federal criminal cases: $20,000 to $100,000+
- Average hourly rate: $100 to $500 (average ~$150/hour)
- DUI cases typically: $1,500 to $5,000 flat fee

Source: Zabrisk, Thumbtack, MTV Law, Cibula Law, Scheuerman Law

**Practical per-case revenue for a small criminal defense firm:** $2,000 to $15,000 for most cases. A misdemeanor DUI generates $2,000-$5,000. A felony generates $10,000-$50,000. Planning assumption for small firm average: $4,000 to $8,000 per case.

## Intake Conversion Rates and Lead-to-Client Ratios

### Website conversion rate (visitor to lead):
- Average law firm website: 2-4% of visitors submit a form or call
- Optimized law firm website (8-12% range): top performers per LEXGRO and Epic Attorney Marketing
- Paid search landing pages: 8.3% median
- Planning assumption for a newly optimized small firm site: 4-6% conversion rate

### Lead-to-consultation rate:
- Not all form submissions are qualified leads
- Roughly 50-60% of form submissions schedule a consultation
- Speed to lead matters: firms responding within 5 minutes convert at 4x the rate of firms responding in 60 minutes+
- Source: intake.link, martindale-avvo.com intake gaps research, Walker Advertising

### Consultation-to-retained client rate:
- Law firm lead conversion average: ~14% from lead to retained client
- Top firms achieve: up to 40% (LEXGRO benchmarks)
- Planning assumption for a competent intake process: 20-25% retained per consultation

### Combined funnel math (conservative):
100 website visitors
x 4% conversion = 4 form submissions
x 55% schedule consultation = 2.2 consultations
x 22% retained = ~0.5 new clients per 100 visitors

For a firm getting 1,000 monthly visitors: 5 new clients per month (conservative)
For a firm getting 2,000 monthly visitors at 6% conversion and 25% retention: 30 new clients per month (top performer)

## Cost Per Lead Benchmarks

### Organic / SEO-driven leads:
- Cost per lead via SEO: $100-$300 for personal injury (First Page Sage estimates for law firm SEO)
- Cost per lead via SEO for general practice: $50-$150
- SEO leads are 5-10x cheaper than PPC leads over 12+ months

### PPC / Google Ads cost per lead:
- Personal injury auto accident: $391 average CPL (2025 data, 49 firms)
- Slip and fall: $312 average CPL
- Workplace injury: $354 average CPL
- Product liability: $476 average CPL
- Medical malpractice: $512 average CPL
- Regional variation: Northeast markets average $468 CPL vs. $314 in Midwest
- CPC for "car accident lawyer": $150-$300 per click in most markets; $300-$500+ in Los Angeles/Chicago
- Monthly budget for PPC: small firms $2,500-$15,000; large multi-market firms $25,000-$100,000+

Source: iLawyer Marketing, Majux, First Page Sage, Taqtics, DM Law Partners (2025-2026 data)

## ROI Math: What One New Case Is Worth

**Conservative scenario: Small PI firm, organic search, 1,000 visitors/month**

Monthly visitors: 1,000
Website conversion rate: 4% = 40 leads
Consultation rate: 55% = 22 consultations
Close rate: 20% = 4.4 new clients/month
Average attorney revenue per PI case: $13,000
Monthly revenue from website: 4.4 x $13,000 = $57,200

If a new optimized website costs $6,000 one-time and $500/month maintenance, it pays for itself in week one of month one assuming this funnel.

**Aggressive scenario: Mid-size PI firm, SEO + optimized site, 3,000 visitors/month**

Monthly visitors: 3,000
Conversion rate: 6% = 180 leads
Consultation rate: 55% = 99 consultations
Close rate: 25% = ~25 new clients/month
Average attorney revenue per case: $13,000
Monthly revenue: 25 x $13,000 = $325,000

Against a $12,000 website build and $2,000/month SEO retainer, this is a 1,300% monthly ROI at steady state.

**Even the smallest scenario:**
One new PI case per month from a website that costs $6,000 to build = payback in one case.
One new divorce case ($8,000 attorney fee) per month from a $5,000 website = payback in one case.
One new felony criminal defense case ($12,000 retainer) from a $6,000 website = payback in one case.
