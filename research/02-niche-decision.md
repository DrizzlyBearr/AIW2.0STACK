## Niche definition (for factory use)

**Client profile (who Katleho sells to):**
- Sales agency or sales infrastructure company, 2-15 people
- $300K-$3M ARR, founder still deeply involved in delivery
- Currently closes through referrals and relationships, no website that converts cold traffic
- Needs a credibility asset for outbound support and inbound discovery
- Will pay $3,000-$8,000 for a purpose-built site that reflects their positioning

**End customer profile (who the client's website must convert):**
- Founder or SMB owner, $500K-$10M revenue, product-market fit established
- Stuck doing every sales call themselves or just burned a bad first sales hire
- Fear: "another agency that won't understand my business"
- Decision moment: revenue plateau, failed hire, or growth mandate from an investor
- Looking for proof: specific numbers, client names, people who look like them

---

## Differentiator the factory must bake in

Every sales agency website Katleho builds must lead with the ROI case, not design quality:
- Case studies with specific numbers (MRR before/after, timeframe, client industry)
- Risk-reversal language that addresses the "burned before" objection
- A clear "who this is for / who this is not for" section
- Pricing transparency or a "typical engagement starts at" anchor
